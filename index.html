Before you ask

The questions other
Lithia rooftops asked first.

Are you a broker?

No. AHX is a marketplace — you contract directly with the carrier and can see exactly what they are paid. The AHX fee is fixed and visible on every load.

Do we have to stop using our current broker?

No — keep doing exactly what you do today. Most stores start by moving a handful of lanes over and putting the results next to their current provider. Once they see the speed, the pricing and the visibility on AHX for themselves, more of their freight tends to end up here.

Who is actually touching my vehicles?

Vetted carriers only. Authority and insurance are monitored continuously, not verified once at signup — and you choose which carrier takes each load.

What if my lane doesn’t get booked?

You can raise your posted price or your rep can work the lane directly. You are not charged for a load that does not move.

How does billing work?

One invoice from AHX per load, covering the carrier rate plus the fixed AHX fee. AHX pays the carrier on your behalf, so there is no separate carrier paperwork on your side.

What about damage claims?

Every carrier on the platform carries active cargo insurance, verified continuously. Condition is documented at pickup and delivery through the tracking integration, and your rep works the claim with you.

Are we set up already, or do we need approval?

AHX is an approved Lithia vendor. Individual stores still need their own account, which takes a few minutes to open.
